---

---

---

### **2ï¸âƒ£ Session ID**

A Session ID is a unique code that identifies your current visit to a website.

Its purpose:

* to know which user is active
* to keep you logged in temporarily
* to track your activity for that session only

When the session expires, you must log in again.

---

### **3ï¸âƒ£ Tokens**

A Token is a secure digital key or proof of identity used in modern authentication systems.

Types used in Google login:

* **ID Token** â†’ proves your identity
* **Access Token** â†’ grants permission to access your Google data
* **Refresh Token** â†’ renews access when the token expires

Tokens are more secure than simple session IDs.

---

### **1ï¸âƒ£ Cookies**

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser.
They are used to:

* remember your login
* store your preferences
* keep you signed in
* track your active session

**Example:**
You stay logged into YouTube because the cookie stores your login info.

---
* **Session ID:** Identifies your active session.
* **Tokens:** Secure identity and access keys.
* **Cookies:** Store login and preferences.
